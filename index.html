Hi, I'm Herwig,

I would describe myself as a quite random person, so would others. I don't have a software or IT background (yet). My studies took place at the Maritime Academy of Antwerp to become a captain of a merchant vessel. My previous job was in 3D printing, something completely different. Now making the switch to Java is yet again a jump into the unknown, but I am really looking forward to it.

The things that interest me most are the ones I don't understand. Which is still a lot. I always wonder how do they do this? How can I do that?
In pursuit of my curiosity, I tend to change hobbies every now and then. Going from learning how to dry and cure meat to melting and casting aluminium in a homemade forge. My latest project was working with leather to make fantasy-style armour (and a purse for my mother, but let's focus on the armour). I will not say that I am always successful at the things I try, but I always get a kick when something works for the first time!

Check out the Projects page for some low-res pictures of a couple of my projects I tried over the years.
